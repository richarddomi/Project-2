What are Fats? Fats are a basic food component. They are a dense source of energy. Unlike glucose, it is more difficult for the body to convert fat into energy.

Fats are used as a source of energy when glucose and glycogen sources are low. When excess fat is consumed, the fat is stored along other fats.
