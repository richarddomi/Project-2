What are carbohydrates? Carbohydrates are a basic food component. They consist of sugars, fibers and starches.

Carbohydrates are used as a source of energy. All carbohyrates are converted to glucose, also known as bood-sugar. When excess carbohyrdates are consumed they are converted into glycogen or fat.
